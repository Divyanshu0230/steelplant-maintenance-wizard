# SOP: Vibration Anomaly Response — Rolling Mill Motors

**Document ID:** SOP-RMM-VIB-001
**Equipment Type:** rolling_mill_motor

## Purpose
Standard procedure for responding to vibration anomalies on rolling mill drive motors.

## Trigger Conditions
- Vibration exceeds 5.5 mm/s for 3 consecutive readings
- Vibration increase >30% within 24 hours
- Correlated motor current fluctuation

## Immediate Actions (0-4 hours)
1. Reduce mill speed by 15-20%
2. Notify shift supervisor and maintenance engineer
3. Capture vibration spectrum and motor current trend
4. Check bearing temperature at drive and non-drive ends

## Investigation Steps (4-24 hours)
1. Compare current readings with baseline from last 30 days
2. Review maintenance history for similar patterns
3. Inspect coupling alignment
4. Check lubrication system pressure and flow
5. Reference incident INC-2024-087 for bearing failure pattern

## Escalation Criteria
- Vibration >8 mm/s: CRITICAL — stop equipment
- Temperature >80°C: HIGH — schedule shutdown within 24h
- Bearing noise detected: HIGH — bearing replacement required

## Required Documentation
Log all actions in digital maintenance logbook. Attach vibration spectra to incident report.
