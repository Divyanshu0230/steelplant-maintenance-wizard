# Rolling Mill Motor Maintenance Manual

## Equipment: RM-MOTOR-03
**Type:** Rolling Mill Drive Motor
**Location:** Hot Rolling Mill Line 2

## Overview
The rolling mill motor is a 2500kW AC induction motor driving the primary rolling stand. Normal operating parameters:
- Temperature: 45-65°C
- Vibration: 2.0-4.5 mm/s RMS
- Motor Current: 18-28 A
- Pressure (cooling): 95-110 bar

## Common Failure Modes

### 1. Bearing Wear
**Symptoms:** Elevated vibration (>6 mm/s), increasing motor current, temperature rise
**Root Cause:** Inadequate lubrication, misalignment, contamination
**Corrective Action:** Inspect bearing clearance, replace if play exceeds 0.05mm

### 2. Shaft Misalignment
**Symptoms:** Axial vibration, harmonic patterns in current signature
**Root Cause:** Thermal expansion, foundation settling, coupling wear

### 3. Winding Insulation Degradation
**Symptoms:** Elevated temperature without load increase, insulation resistance drop

## Maintenance Schedule
- Daily: Visual inspection, vibration spot check
- Weekly: Full vibration analysis, temperature monitoring
- Monthly: Bearing lubrication, alignment verification
- Quarterly: Insulation resistance test, complete bearing inspection

## Spare Parts
- BRG-RMM-450: Bearing Assembly Kit (Lead time: 3 days)
- Minimum stock: 2 units

## Safety
Always follow lockout/tagout procedures before maintenance. Motor must cool below 40°C before bearing work.
