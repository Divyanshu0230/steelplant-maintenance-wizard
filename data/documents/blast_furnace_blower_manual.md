# Blast Furnace Blower Maintenance Manual

## Equipment: BF-BLOWER-01
**Type:** Blast Furnace Blower
**Location:** Blast Furnace Area

## Operating Parameters
- Temperature: 40-60°C
- Vibration: 1.5-3.5 mm/s
- Pressure: 95-115 bar
- Motor Current: 15-25 A

## Maintenance Procedures
Regular inspection of impeller blades, bearing lubrication every 500 operating hours, and seal replacement at 2000 hours.

## Failure Modes
Impeller erosion, bearing failure, seal leakage, motor overheating.

## Critical Notes
Blower failure causes immediate blast furnace production impact. Classified as CRITICAL equipment.
