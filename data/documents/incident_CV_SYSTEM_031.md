# Failure Analysis Report — INC-2025-031

**Equipment:** CV-SYSTEM-12 (Conveyor System 12)
**Date:** 2025-05-20
**Severity:** Medium
**Downtime:** 8.0 hours

## Incident Summary
Belt misalignment caused material spillage and emergency stop of raw material feed to blast furnace.

## Root Cause Analysis
**Primary Cause:** Uneven belt tension after splice repair
**Contributing Factors:**
1. Tracking roller not adjusted post-repair
2. Overload from wet ore batch increased drift
3. Motor thermal margin reduced in summer ambient

## Corrective Actions
1. Re-tensioned belt and aligned tracking rollers
2. Cleared spillage and inspected drive coupling
3. Updated SOP to require alignment check after any splice work

## Recommendations
Monitor motor current and temperature together — thermal events often precede belt drift on overloaded conveyors.
